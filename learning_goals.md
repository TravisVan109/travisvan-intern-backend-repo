- Improving my backend development skills
- Writing cleaner, more efficient, and maintainable code
- Understanding how to work with APIs and databases in real projects
- Learning better debugging and problem-solving techniques
- Improving communication skills, especially in a remote team

## What I Can Contribute to the Team

I can contribute by:

- Taking initiative to learn and complete tasks
- Bringing a strong work ethic and willingness to improve
- Supporting the team by writing clean and structured code
- Communicating clearly about my progress, blockers, or questions
- Being reliable, respectful, and eager to help wherever needed

## One Area I Feel Less Confident In (But Want to Improve)

I would like to improve my confidence in:

- Understanding new codebases quickly
- Breaking down tasks into smaller steps
- Communicating blockers early instead of trying to solve everything alone

These are areas I want to work on throughout the internship.

## What Success in This Internship Looks Like for Me

Success for me means:

- Becoming more confident and independent with backend development
- Understanding Focus Bear’s workflows and contributing meaningful work
- Communicating effectively with the team
- Making steady progress, learning new skills, and improving each week
- Finishing the internship feeling proud of what I have achieved and how I grew professionally
