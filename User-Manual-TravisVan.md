- Standard working hours (with flexibility depending on tasks and meetings)

# How I Work Best

I do my best work when:

- I have **clear instructions or goals** for a task.
- I have some time for **deep work without interruptions**.

I struggle when:

- Requirements or expectations are unclear.
- There are many tasks switching at the same time.
- Important information is missing or scattered across multiple places.

# Communication Style

I prefer messages to be:

- **Clear and direct**, with enough context so I understand the task or issue.
- Written messages are helpful because I can review them again later.

The best way to reach me is:

- **Discord, email, team messaging tools or any contact through my phone number** for quick communication.
- **Scheduled meetings** if a topic needs detailed discussion.

If you need something urgent:

- Send me a **direct message and mention that it is urgent**, and I will try to respond as soon as possible.

# Collaboration & Teamwork

I work best in a team when:

- Everyone communicates clearly and respectfully.
- Tasks and responsibilities are well defined.
- Team members support each other and share knowledge.

My biggest pet peeve in teamwork is:

- Unclear communication or missing information that blocks progress.

If I seem quiet in a meeting, it usually means:

- I am **listening and processing the discussion**, or thinking about the problem before responding.

# Focus & Productivity

I stay focused by:

- Breaking tasks into smaller steps.
- Working in **structured sessions of focused work**.
- Keeping a simple to-do list for the day.

Things that distract me:

- Frequent interruptions.
- Switching between many tasks quickly.

The best way to support my productivity is:

- Provide clear task expectations.
- Allow time for **focused work on one task at a time**.
