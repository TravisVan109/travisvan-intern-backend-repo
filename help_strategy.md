# When to Use Google, AI Tools, or Ask for Help?

### 1. When do you prefer using AI vs. searching Google?
AI is useful when you need quick explanations, examples, or help understanding code. Google is better for official documentation, deeper articles, or debugging specific error messages from real-world cases.

### 2. How do you decide when to ask a colleague instead?
You ask a colleague when you're blocked for too long, the issue is related to team-specific knowledge, or the answer affects shared systems or code standards. It saves time and avoids misunderstandings.

### 3. What challenges do developers face when troubleshooting alone?
Developers may miss obvious mistakes, waste time exploring wrong directions, or become stuck due to lack of context. Troubleshooting alone can also lead to frustration and slower progress.
